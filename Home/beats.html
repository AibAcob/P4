When the sun goes down

Produced by: RJBeatz and AIB

140 BPM- Key: C Minor - This beat was made within a dark atmosphere. From the melodies, countermelodies, and the drums, the purpose of the beat was to make vibe and ambience of the song ominous.

No More Reasons

Produced by: AIB

94 BPM - Key: Eb Minor - This beat comes from a sample from an Isaiah Rashad and Mick Jenkins drum kit. It was meant to be a mid tempo west coast beat but turned out completely different from the original idea. The classic feel of the beat calls for smooth vocals and melodic flows.

Dreams

Produced by: RJBeatz and AIB

120 BPM- Key: A Minor - Dreams was made on a lazy rainy day. The guitar was a sample found on a royalty free website. The drums were laid out and the entire beat was left alone for a few hours. The flute was then added as a counter melody and by that point, the entire state of New Jersey was under water. The beat was not saved into system for an entire 12 hours.
